Since my dad owned an internet cafe in Rowland Heights, CA, I've found myself playing PC games as far as I can remember. Creating battle simulators on Blizzard's Warcraft III World Editor, role-playing with childhood friends, and playing "golfkey" (golf and hockey) with my brother, at the time, were my proudest game design achievements.

During college, I learned to program and build games on C++, C#, and Python. I've built an engine from scratch once using SDL OpenGL, constructed two VR games, and even shipped a fully networked game for an online graduates course at my school.

Building things and watching them interact with others has always been a passion of mine and I would love to connect with others who share this same passion!
